# ===============
# Movie Poster
# ===============

Create a hyper-realistic 4K photo poster collage featuring the same man 
from the provided reference image, keeping his face 100% identical with no 
alterations. Include three poses: (1) an intense close-up portrait, 
(2) a relaxed full-body pose, and a turning side-profile shot. 
The man wears a red leather jacket, a white T-shirt, jeans, 
and white sneakers. Arrange all three poses in a layered collage composition over a smokey waves.
Add subtle typography watermark at the bottom-center 
reading: “Created By : ALOK KUMAR KAUSHAL” — small but readable, 
neon orange glowing text with a soft outer glow. Apply an MS-Office-style outer border stroke 
using a smooth Blue → Green gradient, luminous, clean, modern, not overpowering the artwork.
