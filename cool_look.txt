# ==========
# Cool Look
# ==========

A hyper-realistic 8k medium shot captured with a straight-on camera angle 
and a slightly wide lens. The main subject stands in the foreground with arms crossed, 
looking directly into the camera with an intense, confident expression; 
uploaded 100% face is used as reference. They wear a highly detailed black 
leather biker jacket with silver zippers and snaps over a light grey crew-neck t-shirt, 
showcasing rich textures, natural folds, and realistic material depth. 
Cinematic lighting with a soft frontal key light gently illuminates the face and jacket, 
creating subtle shadows that enhance the realism.
Behind the subject is a dense, wall-like collage of black-and-white portrait photos of the same person. 
The portraits are arranged in overlapping, slightly tilted layers, 
forming a textured monochrome backdrop. These background images are sharp yet 
rendered in slightly softer focus than the subject to enhance depth of field. 
The main subject is in full color while the collage is entirely monochrome, 
following a “Photoshop logic” contrast that emphasizes the foreground presence and creates a striking visual separation.
Add subtle typography watermark at the bottom-center 
reading: “Created By : ALOK KUMAR KAUSHAL” — small but readable, 
neon orange glowing text with a soft outer glow. Apply an MS-Office-style outer border stroke 
using a smooth Blue → Green gradient, luminous, clean, modern, not overpowering the artwork.
